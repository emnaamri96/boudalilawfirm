what we do

our practice areas

corporate law

Corporate law is an area of law that deals with the legal structure and governance of corporations, limited liability companies, partnerships, and other forms of business entities. As a law firm specializing in corporate law, we are well-equipped to provide a range of legal services to our clients. One of our key services is the formation of corporations and other business entities. This includes advising clients on the most appropriate form of business for their needs, drafting and filing the necessary paperwork, and assisting with the obtaining of any necessary licenses or permits. We also provide ongoing legal support to our clients in areas such as corporate governance, regulatory compliance, and contract negotiation and drafting. Our expertise in these areas allows us to provide guidance to companies on a variety of legal matters and help them navigate the complex legal landscape. We also have a strong track record in representing clients in mergers and acquisitions, both as buyers and sellers. Our team is experienced in negotiating and drafting the necessary agreements, and advising clients on the various legal and financial considerations involved in these transactions. In addition, we offer a range of dispute resolution services to our clients, including representation in arbitration and litigation proceedings. Our attorneys have extensive experience in representing clients in both commercial and corporate disputes, and are equipped to provide effective representation and advocacy on their behalf. At our law firm, we are dedicated to providing our clients with the highest quality legal services and expert guidance in the area of corporate law. Whether you are a start-up company looking to form a new business entity, or an established corporation in need of legal support, we are here to help

mining law

Mining is a complex and highly regulated industry that requires specialized legal support. As a law firm with experience in this field, we are well-positioned to provide a range of legal services to companies operating in the mining sector. One of our key services is advising clients on the complex legal and regulatory environment that governs the mining industry. This includes guidance on environmental, health and safety regulations, as well as labor and employment law. We also assist with the obtaining of mining licenses and permits, and advise clients on the rights and obligations associated with these permits. We also provide ongoing legal support in areas such as contract negotiation and drafting, dispute resolution, and corporate governance. Our expertise in these areas allows us to provide comprehensive legal services to companies operating in the mining sector, helping them to navigate the complex legal landscape and achieve their business objectives. We have extensive experience representing clients in negotiations and disputes related to mining rights, royalties, and other financial arrangements. Our attorneys are equipped to provide effective representation and advocacy in these matters, working to protect our clients' interests and resolve disputes in a timely and efficient manner. In addition, we offer a range of services related to mining finance, including representation in connection with the negotiation and closing of mining project financing arrangements, and the drafting and negotiation of mining project joint venture agreements. At our law firm, we are committed to providing our clients in the mining industry with the highest quality legal services and expert guidance. Whether you are a mining company looking to obtain a new permit, or are facing a complex legal issue, we are here to help

gas & oil

At Boudali Law Firm, we have extensive experience in the field of oil and gas law, providing legal advice and support to a wide range of clients, including investors and explorers. Our team of highly skilled lawyers has a deep understanding of the complex legal and regulatory environment surrounding the oil and gas industry, and is committed to providing clients with expert guidance and support. We offer a full range of legal services to companies operating in the oil and gas sector, including contract negotiation, regulatory compliance, and dispute resolution. Our attorneys have a wealth of experience in negotiating and drafting exploration and production contracts, joint venture agreements, and other key commercial documents. We also advise clients on the compliance of their operations with local, national, and international laws and regulations, ensuring that they remain in good standing with all relevant authorities. In addition, our team is equipped to handle any disputes that may arise in the course of a client's operations. Whether it is a commercial dispute with a business partner or a regulatory issue with a government agency, our attorneys have the experience and knowledge to help clients navigate the legal process and achieve the best possible outcome. Overall, we are dedicated to providing our clients in the oil and gas industry with the highest level of legal services and support. With a focus on professionalism, integrity, and excellence, we strive to be a trusted advisor and partner to our clients in this dynamic and fast-paced industry. If you are looking for a law firm with the expertise and experience to help you succeed in the oil and gas industry, please contact us to learn more about our services.

Arbitration

Our law firm provides specialized services in the field of arbitration. We understand that disputes can arise in any business, and it's essential to have an experienced and knowledgeable team by your side to help you navigate these challenging situations. That's why we offer comprehensive support to our clients during arbitration disputes and even after that, with the exequatur of the arbitration awards. We have extensive experience in representing clients in both domestic and international arbitration proceedings, and we have a deep understanding of the complexities involved in these cases. Our team of experts has a proven track record of successfully navigating these complex cases and securing favorable outcomes for our clients. When representing our clients during an arbitration dispute, we work closely with them to understand their specific needs and concerns, and we use our extensive knowledge of the law and our litigation skills to represent them in the best possible way. We also provide comprehensive support throughout the entire arbitration process, from filing the initial claim to preparing for the hearing and advocating for our client's rights and interests. After the arbitration award has been issued, we can also assist with its exequatur, ensuring that it is recognized and enforced in the relevant jurisdiction. We have a thorough understanding of the legal requirements for the exequatur of arbitration awards and can provide our clients with the support and guidance they need to secure the recognition and enforcement of the award. In conclusion, our law firm is committed to providing top-notch legal services in the field of arbitration. We understand the importance of resolving disputes effectively and efficiently, and we are dedicated to helping our clients achieve the best possible outcome in their case. If you're facing an arbitration dispute, we're here to help
