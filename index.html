O U R H I S T O R Y

Our law firm has a rich history dating back to 1960 when it was founded by Maître Noureddine BOUDALI. Maître BOUDALI was a proud member of the first generation of the CAPA and made a name for himself by handling many high-profile cases, including the honor of pleading before the leader of Tunisia, Habib BOURGUIBA. After Maître BOUDALI passed away, his son Maître Makram BOUDALI took over the private practice and continued to build upon its legacy. Maître Makram BOUDALI specialized in corporate law and worked tirelessly to provide the best legal representation to his clients. In 2023, Maître Makram BOUDALI took the next step in the evolution of the law firm by turning the private practice into a full-fledged law firm. Today, our law firm continues to operate with the same principles of excellence and commitment to justice that were established by its founder, Maître Noureddine BOUDALI

Our Billing Policy
For Billable clients, we charge according to the hourly rate published in our website. The calculation of our fees is automated via our software taking in consideration tasks performed, the tasker's hourly fees and urgency.

We pledge to provide our clients with a simulation predicting, with 90% accuracy, the foreseen fees for the services demanded. Whenever we approach the estimated amount, we pledge to seek the client's authorization before advancing any further. This does not apply to urgent cases where our intervention is critical to protect the client's interests from an emanant threat.

Please note that expenses are billed separately.
We finally pledge to treat our clients with fairness and transparency.
